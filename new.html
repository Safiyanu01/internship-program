SAVE A LIFE PROJECT

Save a life Project is dedicated to giving the underpriviledg Children chance to live normal lives through Quality education, empowerment, and development. Hundreds of children are orphaned or abandoned and left hungry, homeless, and without any opportunities for education. Mortality rates among children are high in many countries with many children as well as adults suffering and dying from hunger, diseases, and some due to gender inequality.


Mission

To explore innovative solutions that will transform communities through improved equity and excellence in education.

Vision

To secure the right to education for less privileged Children In Nigeria.

Values

Integrity, Inclusion, Impact

Who we are

Youths Energy Organization activities are geared towards creating educational opportunities for under-privileged children across Nigeria through our scholarship program, community cluster learning model, advocacy and stakeholder engagement, livelihood support and psychosocial program.
